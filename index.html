Rules & Methodology

7 Confirmation Triggers (v5)

All 7 required for A+ | 5/7 + Kill Zone = A | 4/7 min = B | Below 4 = NO TRADE

  • ✅ HTF bias confirmed — Monthly + Weekly + Daily aligned
  • ✅ Price in deep Q1 (long) or Q4 (short) of HTF dealing range
  • ✅ Key liquidity COMPLETED sweep (BSL or SSL) — not anticipated, actually swept
  • ✅ FVG or OB formed on 1m-5m AFTER the sweep
  • ✅ 1m CHoCH or MSS confirmed in setup direction (NON-NEGOTIABLE)
  • ✅ ICT Macro or Silver Bullet window active (18:50 GST AM / 22:50 GST PM)
  • ✅ R:R ≥ 1:3 to nearest HTF liquidity target

Graded Risk

  • A+ (7/7+Macro): risk 2% of account · stop tight · deploy on alignment
  • A (5/7+KZ): risk 1.5% · bread-and-butter · most trades land here
  • B (4/7 min): risk 1% · clean LTF scalp only · small size

Kill Zones (GST)

  • 🟢 London KZ: 08:00–10:00 GST
  • 🟢 NY AM KZ: 13:00–15:00 GST
  • ⚡ ICT Macro AM: 18:50 GST
  • 🔴 Dead Zone: 19:00–22:00 GST (NO NEW TRADES)
  • ⚡ Silver Bullet PM: 22:50 GST
  • ⛔ Hard cutoff: 23:30 GST

⭐ Golden Rules — Never Break These

  • Trade only graded setups (A+/A/B). If you have to convince yourself, it isn't one.
  • 1m CHoCH/MSS is NON-NEGOTIABLE. No CHoCH = no trade. Period.
  • Size is not the lever — quality is. One A+ beats ten B-grade trades.
  • Spot is a swing game. Checking prices hourly = gambling.
  • One red day = half size next day. Two red days = full day off.
  • Process > Outcome. A losing trade taken well is still a good trade.

⚠ NO TRADE CONDITIONS

Missing 1m CHoCH · R:R below 1:2 · News in 30min · Dead Zone 19:00-22:00 GST · 2 losses today · Daily limit hit
